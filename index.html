What Babji does

From day one, the assistant is meant to learn useful routines, not send generic noise.

Babji combines tasks, reminders, wellness check-ins, and routine signals to generate context-aware support that feels grounded in the user’s actual day.

How it helps

  • Personalized morning prompts instead of fixed generic notifications
  • Predictive nightly closeout based on learned routine timing
  • Goal-aware reminders, task prioritization, and routine support
  • User-controlled consent for learning and background signals

Morning

Babji waits for the start of the user’s day, then sends one clear prompt after a short delay.

Midday

The assistant helps organize tasks, reminders, nutrition, and context-aware actions.

Night

Babji closes the day with reflection and tomorrow’s top focus, rather than broadcasting generic motivation.

Privacy and transparency

Babji is built around explicit consent.

Features that rely on learning, Screen Time, notifications, or location are opt-in and designed to be transparent. Users can review settings, disable background features, and manage permissions through iOS and in-app controls.
